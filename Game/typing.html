Typing Speed Game

A typing speed game is a computer program or online game that tests and measures a person's typing speed and accuracy. These games usually involve typing out a series of words or sentences as quickly and accurately as possible within a certain time limit. The player's typing speed is then measured in words per minute (WPM) or characters per minute (CPM).
