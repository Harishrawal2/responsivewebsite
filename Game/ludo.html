Write Sentace and Listen to the sentance

A voice sentence game is a computer program or online game that tests and measures a person's ability to listen to and accurately transcribe spoken sentences. These games typically involve listening to a series of spoken sentences and typing out the words that are spoken as accurately as possible within a certain time limit. The player's accuracy and speed in transcribing the spoken sentences is then measured and scored.
